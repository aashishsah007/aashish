Position Sizing Rule

Pro traders kabhi bhi ek trade mein 2% se zyada risk nahi lete.

Formula:

Max Risk = Capital × Risk%
SL Points = Entry - Stop Loss
Units = Max Risk ÷ SL Points
Lots = Units ÷ Lot Size

⚠️ Agar Capital ₹1L hai toh ek trade mein sirf ₹1000-2000 risk karo.

Pivot Points Use Kaise Karein
📌 Pivot Point (PP) = Din ka most important level. PP ke upar = Bullish bias, PP ke neeche = Bearish bias.
📌 R1, R2, R3 = Intraday resistance levels. Yahan se price wapas aa sakti hai. Short entry ke liye dekhو.
📌 S1, S2, S3 = Intraday support levels. Yahan se bounce ho sakta hai. Long entry ke liye dekho.
💡 Pro tip: Jab price R1 todta hai toh R2 target banta hai. S1 toota toh S2 target.

Event Pe Trade Kaise Karein
⚠️ Event se pehle
Market ka "expectation" samjho. Agar sab bullish soch rahe hain aur result average aaya, toh bhi market gir sakta hai ("buy the rumor, sell the news").
📌 Event ke din
Size chhoti rakho. High volatility mein bade lots dangerous hote hain. Stop loss zaroori hai.
✅ Event ke baad
Direction confirm hone ke baad trade lo. Pehle dust settle hone do.
💡 Expiry week
Option buyers ke liye risky. Theta decay bahut fast hota hai. Sellers ka advantage hota hai.
